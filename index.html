Calling all chess enthusiasts in Trim! Gather in the heart of this historic town and put your strategic skills to the test at the Trim Library Chess Club. This friendly meetup welcomes players of all chess levels. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a curious beginner, come enjoy a game, learn from others, and be part of a vibrant community passionate about the timeless strategy of chess.

About

Hone your strategic mind and connect with fellow chess lovers at the Trim Library Chess Club. This welcoming group caters to all, offering friendly competition and a chance to sharpen your skills. Twice a year, the club puts their mettle to the test in regional tournaments, while also hosting monthly local competitions to keep the competitive spirit high.
